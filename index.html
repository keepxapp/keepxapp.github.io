Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about KeepX? Check out our FAQ section to find answers to common queries.

How does KeepX ensure the security of my passwords?

Your passwords are encrypted using your own encryption key, which is known only to you. This ensures that only you can access your data.

Can I access my passwords on multiple devices?

Yes, KeepX syncs your data in real-time across all your devices, so you can access your passwords wherever you are.

What happens if I lose my encryption key?

Since KeepX uses end-to-end encryption with a key that only you know, losing your encryption key means you cannot access your passwords. It's important to keep your key secure.
